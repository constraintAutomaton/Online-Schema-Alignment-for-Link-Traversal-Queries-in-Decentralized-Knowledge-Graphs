\begin{abstract}
The integration of heterogeneous knowledge graphes remains challenging, particularly in decentralized environments where discovery queries execute over distributed, unindexed sources requiring alignment rules for unified querying.
This demo paper presents an online schema alignment approach integrated into a \glsentrylong{ltqp} engine.
A social media scenario demonstrates querying across networks with varying vocabularies.
The web-based interface enables intuitive exploration and seamless cross-schema querying.
Source code, library, and command-line interface are provided for integration into other applications.
\end{abstract}
